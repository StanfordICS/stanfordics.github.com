Aspiring to continue your education in the United States? Not sure which tests to take, which schools to apply, what to do... Application to US universities can be extremely confusing - we know - but we're here to help :-)
Come join us at @america Auditorium, Pacific Place Mall Jakarta, Indonesia, on January 7, 2012 from 1 - 5 pm! Hear talks about application process and life in the US, and sign up for 1-on-1 (not literally - you can bring your parents too) mentoring session with students and alumni from top US universities.
11th grade and younger students are highly encouraged to come - it's not too early to understand the application process better! Masters and PhD applicants are also welcomed.

Mentoring Sessions 3-5 pm • registration-only

Get personalized advice from people who have been in your shoes! We'll match you up with US College students / alumni that we think understand your needs best.
