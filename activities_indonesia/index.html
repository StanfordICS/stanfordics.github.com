Aspiring to continue your education in the United States? Not sure which tests to take, which schools to apply, what to do... Application to US universities can be extremely confusing - we know - but we're here to help! Come join us at @america Auditorium, Pacific Place Mall Jakarta, Indonesia, on January 7, 2012 from 1 - 5 pm. Hear talks about application process and life in the US, and sign up for 1-on-1 (not literally - you can bring your parents too) mentoring session with students and alumni from top US universities.
